Let's KEEP the SEA trash FREE!

Coastal Cleanups Count

Coastal cleanups protect wildlife, keep our waters healthier, and make shoreline spaces safer and more enjoyable for everyone. By removing trash before it washes into rivers and oceans, volunteers help prevent animals from getting tangled in debris or mistaking plastic for food. Each cleanup also raises community awareness, inspiring everyday choices that help reduce pollution at the source.

What to Expect

Helping hands, cleaner shores, and a clearer picture of our impact.

  • Checkup: Check in with event leaders, pick up gloves and bags, and get a quick safety and orientation talk before heading out.
  • Cleanup: Walk the shoreline in small teams, collecting trash and recording unusual items to help track pollution trends.
  • Wrapup: Wrap up by tallying bags and debris, sharing any interesting finds, and snapping a group photo to celebrate your collective impact.
